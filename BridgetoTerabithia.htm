Bridge to Terabithia


Author: Katherine Paterson

Published: 1977

Genre: Drama/Comedy

AR Points: 5

Publisher's Website: Crowell

Description: Jesse Aarons is a boy who lives in rural, Southwest Virginia with his family. He meets a new girl who just moved to his town and they become good friends. Leslie is her name and they both really enjoy telling stories and drawing. They create an imaginary world and have adventures in this magical land.

  • Inside Scoop: "This is a classy novel about friendship and growing up. I was very entertained." - Von Wiltz
