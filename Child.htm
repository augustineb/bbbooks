A Child Called "It"

Authors: Dave Pelzer

Published: Omaha Press 1993

Genre: Non-Fiction

AR Points: 5

Author Website: Dave Pelzer

Description: It’s about a little boy that is abused by this mother. He has to sleep in the garage and do many different things that hurt him. His mom is a very mean and cruel woman. His dad left them because he was sick of it.

  • Inside Scoop: "Yes, It's called A Child Called It because after a while his mom just starts calling him "it." She said he doesn't deserve to have a name because he isn't a person. She was very mean." - Kenzi Holt
