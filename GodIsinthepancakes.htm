God Is In the Pancakes

Author: Robin Epstein

Published: 2010

Genre: Drama

AR Points: 10

Author's Website: Robin Epstein

Description: Weird title- Really good story. Grace's family life is changing, and so is her relationship with her best friend forever, Eric. The best part of her life, then, was her part-time job at the nursing home - until her favorite patient asks her to help him die.

  • The Inside Scoop: "I love how this author makes us feel the sweet relationship between this young girl and the elderly man and forces us to take a look at complitcated end-of-life issues." - Mary Napier
