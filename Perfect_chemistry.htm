Perfect Chemistry

Author: Simone Elkeles

Published: December 23, 2008

Genre: Romance, drama

AR Points: 13

Author's Website: Simone Elkeles

Description: When Brittany Ellis walks into chemistry class on the first day of senior year, she has no clue that her carefully created "perfect" life is about to unravel before her eyes. She's forced to be lab partners with Alex Fuentes, a gang member from the other side of town, and he is about to threaten everything she's worked so hard for--her flawless reputation, her relationship with her boyfriend, and the secret that her home life is anything but perfect...There is something about Alex Fuentes, that none of the other boys have! Is Brittany really going to fall for this so-called badboy?

  • The Inside Scoop: "This is one of my favorite books! It's a good story about two people from totally different lives, that find love when they least expect it and when everyone is against them! A perfect story for people who like love and drama..." - Jenny Monn-Iversen
