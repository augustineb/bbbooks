Alanna

Author: Tamora Pierce

Published: 1983

Genre: Adventure, Fantasy/Sci-Fi

AR Points: 7

Description: Alanna of Trebond does not want to be sent away to learn to be a lady any more than her brother Thom wants to be a knight. However, this is what their father, Alan of Trebond, a man absorbed completely in his studies since his wife's death, has ordered for them. But Alanna and Thom will take matters into their own hands.

  • Inside Scoop: "A nice paced, good read for those who like adventure and fantasy with just a hint of romance." - Greg Garner
