Queen of Babble gets Hitched

Author: Meg Cabot

Published: 2008

Genre: Romance, Comedy

AR Points: 12

Author's Website: Meg Cabot

Description: It's about this girl who always gets in trouble by blabbing stuff she shouldn't say out loud. She moves to New York and is finally starting her own wedding dress business and ends up breaking off her engagement and marries her true love from college, who is also her ex-finance's best friend.

  • The Inside Scoop: "It's a fast read and very entertaining. It doesn't ever really get boring. It's filled with real life drama and that always keeps people's attention. It's the kind of book that makes you want to follow your dreams and it inspires you to do so." - Jami Rafferty
