Bleach

Author: Tite Kubo

Published: 2001

Genre: Comic/Manga, Action/Adventure

AR Points: None

Publisher's Website: Viz Media

Description: Ichigo Kurosaki was never a normal teenage guy - he could see ghosts. But his life is about to take a turn for the even more bizzare when he accidentally absorbs all the power of a Soul Reaper and becomes one, himself. Now he must face all sorts of evil undead spirits. Good thing he's not alone.
