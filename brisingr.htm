Brisingr

Author: Christopher Paolini

Published: September, 2008

Genre: Fantasy/Sci-Fi

AR Points: 45

Author's Website: Christopher Paolini

Description: After discovering that Murtagh is his brother and winning the first fight against the Empire, Eragon goes with his cousin to kill the Ra'zac.
