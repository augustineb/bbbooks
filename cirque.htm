Cirque Du Freak

Author: Darren Shan

Published: 2000

Genre: Thriller

AR Points: 7

Author's Website: Darren Shan

Description: Cirque Du Freak is the frightening saga of a young boy who visits a freak show with his friend. He discovers that one of the performers is a vampire that has led him to the world of the vampires.
