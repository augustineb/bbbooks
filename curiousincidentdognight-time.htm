The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time

Author: Mark Haddon

Published: 2003

Genre: Drama

AR Points: 10

Publisher's Website: Random House

Description: Christoper Boone, a young boy with severe autism, attempts to solve the murder of the neighborhood dog. We see the world through his eyes as he uncovers far more than he had intended.

  • The Inside Scoop: "It's fascinating to see the world through this boy's eyes, since he sees things so differently than I do. It really made me think. Plus the story has some interesting twists." - Mr. O'Halloran
