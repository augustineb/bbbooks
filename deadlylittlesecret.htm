Deadly Little Secret

Author: Laurie Faria Stolarz

Published: 2008

Genre: Romance, Drama, Action/Adventure

AR Points: 7

Author's Website: Laurie Faria Stolarz

Description: After the accident Camelia is trying to find the mysterious boy who pushed her out of the way of a car. When he shows up at her school as a student, rumors fly about his past. After Camelia starts getting strange packages, she realizes she's in danger. But who can she trust?
