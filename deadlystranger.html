Deadly Stranger


Author: Peg Kehret

Published: 1998

Genre: Thriller

AR Points: 5

Publisher's Website: Peg Kehret

Description: A mental patient breaks out of a hospital and kidnaps a girl he thinks is his sister. Then he goes back to kill the new girl in town who saw it happen but thought he was the girl's brother.

  • Inside Scoop: "If you need a few more points and are looking for a short and easy book but with a good story, read this book." - Kimberly Glotzbach
