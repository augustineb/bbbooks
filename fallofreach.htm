The Fall of Reach

Author: Eric Nylund

Published: 2001

Genre: Science Fiction, Action/Adventure

AR Points: 15

Author's Website: Eric Nyland

Description: This book is about the humans' war against a race of aliens known as the covenant. It narrates the life of Master Cheif, a super-soldier known as a Spartan, from the time he ws drafted until the end where he finds Halo.

  • The Inside Scoop: "It is a book that will stick in your hands until you are finished reading it. The book is very entertaining and it gives you the perspective of the difference between races." - Chris McCarthy
