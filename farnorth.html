Far North


Author: Will Hobbs

Published: 1996

Genre: Action/Adventure

AR Points: 9

Publisher's Website: Will Hobbs

Description: Fifteen-year-old Gabe Rogers marvels at the beauty of Canada's Northwest Territories. He hears about the tales of Deadman Valley and a river called the Nahanni. He doesn't know that shortly he will be stranded with his roommate and his roommate's Grandpa in the mysterious valley.

  • Inside Scoop: "A very good book. I would recommend this book to all outdoorsy people" - Tyler Glotzbach
