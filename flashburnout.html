Flash Burnout


Author: L. K. Madigan

Published: 2010

Genre: Romance, Comedy, Tragedy, Drama

AR Points: 9

Publisher's Website: L. K. Madigan

Description: Blake Hewson, Sophomore and photographer, takes a photo of his friend Marissa Fairbairn's mom, whom he hasn't seen in over a year. His hot girlfriend Shannon DeWinters is the best thing to happen to him. This is great until he spends too much time helping Marissa find her meth addict mom, and they end up getting closer than they should.
