Hatchet

Author: Gary Paulsen

Published: December, 2006

Genre: Action/Adventure

AR Points: 7

Publisher's Website: Simon Says

Description: A young boy, Brian Robeson, is going to his dad's for summer. His parents had gotten divorce earlier in his life. His dad lives a long way away so he has to take a plane, a Cessna 406 bushplane, to get there. Something happens to the pilot shortly after they left to his dad's. Brian has to land the plane somewhere in a big forest and figure out where he is and find a way to get back to the city. All he has to survive is a hatchet.

  • The Inside Scoop: This is a very good book about the outdoors. Anyone will enjoy this book if they take the time to read it. The cover may look like it could be a little creepy, but like they say, "Don't judge a book by its cover"!
