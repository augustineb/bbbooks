Holes

Author: Louis Sachar

Published: 1998

Genre: Action/Adventure, Thriller

AR Points: 7

Publisher's Website: Bloomsbury

Description: Stanley Yelnats is a boy that is sent to Camp Green Lake, a boys' detention center, for being unjustly accused of stealing. All the boys have to dig holes exactly five feet wide and five feet deep. None of the boys know why they are digging the holes, but they aim to find out.

  • The Inside Scoop: "I read it before the movie came out. It is a very good book." - Brittany Pearl
