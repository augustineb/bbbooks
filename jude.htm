Jude

Author: Kate Morgenroth

Genre: Thriller

AR Points: 13

Publisher's Website: Simon and Schuster

Description: When the book opens, 17-year-old Jude sees his father murdered. Because he refuses to tell the police what he knows or what he saw, he becomes their prime suspect. His relationships with his mom and step dad add to the drama as the story unfolds.
