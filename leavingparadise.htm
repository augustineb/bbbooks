Leaving Paradise

Author: Simone Elkeles

Published: 2007

Genre: Romance, Drama

AR Points: 9

Author's Website: Simone Elkeles

Description: Nothing has been the same since Caleb Becker left a party drunk, got behind the wheel, and hit Maggie Armstrong. Even after months, she's still crippled, and her social life has taken a dive. After a year in jail, Caleb's free, but everyone treats him differently. The both of them are "outsiders", left by former friends. But they find comfort from a surprising source: each other!
