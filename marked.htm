House of Night Novels: Marked

Author: P.C. and Kristen Cast

Published: 2007

Genre: Romance, Drama, Action/Adventure, Fantasy/Sci-Fi

AR Points: 11

Publisher's Website: St. Martin's Press

Description: After she is Marked, 16-year-old Zoey Redbird enters the House of Night and learns that she is no average fledgling. She has been Marked as special by the vampyre Goddess Nyx. But she is not the only fledgling at the House of Night with special powers. When she discovers that the leader of the Dark Daughters, the school's most elite club, is mis-using her Goddess-given gifts, Zoey must look deep within herself for the courage to embrace her destiny— with a little help from her new vampyre friends.
