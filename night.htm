Night

Auther: Elie Wiesel

Published: 1960

Genre: Non-Fiction

AR Points: 4

Author's Website: Elie Wiesel

Description: A terrifying life at the Nazi death camp that changed a young boy into an agonized witness to the death of his family and the deaths of other families.

  • The Inside Scoop: "Elie Wiesel was a little boy when taken to the Auschwitz concentration camp with very little or no food or water and was still able to live and tell the story in his own words." - Derek Tyler
