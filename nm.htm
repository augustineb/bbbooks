The Twilight Saga: New Moon

Author: Stephenie Meyer

Published: 2006

Genre: Romance, Drama, Action/Adventure

AR Points: 20

Publisher's Website: Hachette Book Group

Description: Bella (from the first book, Twilight) is back with even more adventures in store for her. Now that Edward has done what he did, what is there to live for? Who will heal her broken heart? Jacob is there to help her along but he has a secret. A near death experience may lead to something she fears the most but she has a choice to make.

  • Inside Scoop: "This book can be boring at times, but just keep reading because you will be glad you did once you get to the end. You will be yelling at her to just figure it out already!! It can get very annoying but it's still good." - Jenna Yocum
