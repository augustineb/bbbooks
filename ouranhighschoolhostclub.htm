Ouran High School Host Club

Author: Bisco Hatori

Published: 2002 (in Japan), 2005 (in the US)

Genre: Romance, Comedy, Drama, Comic/Manga

AR Points: 2

Publisher's Website: Shojo Beat

Description: After accidentally breaking a super-expensive vase, Haruhi must join the host club (a group of rich boys entertaining equally rich girls for the fun of it) to pay off the debt. There's only one problem: Haruhi is a girl!
