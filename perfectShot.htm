The Perfect Shot

Author: Elaine Marie Alphin

Published: 2011

Genre: Sports / Mystery

AR Points: 14

Description: Brian’s girlfriend, Amanda, is murdered.  The police think it was her father, but Brian believes otherwise.  Everyone tries to get him to move on and focus on other things, like basketball, but Brian thinks he may have to make a different choice.
