Purple Heart


Author: Patricia McCormick

Published: 2008

Genre: Drama,Tragedy

AR Points: 6

Publisher's Website: Garry Prowe

Description: There's a memory that haunts him; an image of a young Iraqi boy as a bullet hits his chest. Matt can't shake the feeling that he was involved. Because of his head injury he can't remember and is now putting the pieces back together.
