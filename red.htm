Red Midnight

Authors: Ben Mikaelsen

Published: 2002

Genre: Action/Adventure

AR Points: 7

Publisher's Website: Harper Collins

Description: When a neighboring tribe comes into Santiago’s village in Guatemala . They burn all the buildings and kill almost all the villagers. The only survivors in Santiago’s family are he and his 4-year-old sister. It becomes Santiago’s responsibility to take his little sister and sail across the ocean to safety in America.

  • The Inside Scoop: "This is a great adventure story with the 14-year-old boy and his 4-year-old sister battling nature and the ocean for survival." - Mrs. Napier
