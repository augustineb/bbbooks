The Red Pyramid

Author: Rick Riordan

Published: 2010

Genre: Fantasy/Sci-Fi, Action/Adventure

AR Points: 18

Author's Website: Rick Riordan

Description: Carter and Sadie are brother and sister but have lived separately ever since their mother died. On one of the two days they get to be together, their dad summons some Ancient Egytian gods and Carter and Sadie end up hosting two of them. They end up having to save the world from the powers of Chaos and learn about what really happened to their mother.

  • The Inside Scoop: "This book is a great read. You can learn about Ancient Egypt and get to read an awesome adventure story. You should really give this book a try." - Deanna Toenjes
