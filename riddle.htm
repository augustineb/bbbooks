The Riddle

Author: Alison Croggon

Published: 2004

Genre: Romance, Action/Adventure

AR Points: 25

Publisher's Website: Penguin Group

Description: Maerad has discovered she is the Chosen One. The Dark Nameless One is rising in power and only she can stop him. Maerad and her mentor Caduan travel to solve the riddle of the Treesong before all hope is lost.

  • Inside Scoop: "I really enjoy the story. The reading is fairly easy and characters are believed and likeable. One concern for me is the pacing. you can read several pages and not progress in the story or character development." - Mr. Rieschick
