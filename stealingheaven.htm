Stealing Heaven

Author: Elizabeth Scott

Published: 2008

Genre: Drama

AR Points: 8

Author's Website: Elizabeth Scott

Description: Eighteen year-old Dani has grown up learning to be a master thief from the best - her mom. They travel from place to place as they steal, and she starts to feel at home in a place called Heaven, making friends and getting a crush on a cute boy, when she and her mother are caught.
