Storm Breaker

Authors: Anthony Horowitz

Published: 2001

Genre: Thriller

AR Points: 7

Publisher's Website: Fantastic Fiction

Description: A secret agent, Alex Rider, lives with his uncle, Ian Rider, after his mother and father are killed. His skill of being sneaky and being able to get out of difficult places lands him a job. At the age of 14 he becomes the guy that MI6 goes to if they have a problem. He gets into the enemy's building and that's when everything goes wrong. This is the first book in the Alex Rider adventure series.

  • The Inside Scoop: "This story is very thrilling and will always keep you on your toes. You never know what is going to happen next. Once you read one you just have to keep on reading the rest in the series." - Ethan Fank
