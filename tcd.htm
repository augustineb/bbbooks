The Captain's Dog

Author: Roland Smith

Published:1999

Genre: Historical Fiction

AR Points: 8

Author's Website: Roland Smith

Description: This book is written in diary form, but the dog is the narrator. It depicts the Lewis and Clark Expedition and the journeys and struggles along the way.

  • The Inside Scoop: "You may think that this book will not be exciting because it is a book about history, but you would be wrong. This book is filled with action and entertainment. It puts a twist on a history lesson that you may have already learned." - Eric Wietharn
