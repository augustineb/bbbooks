The Gospel According to Larry

Author: Janet Tashjian

Published: 2001

Genre: Comedy

AR Points: 5

Publisher's Website: MacMillan

Description: Josh Swenson, 17 and highly intelligent, has a lot to say. He wants to change the world, so he posts his opinions in the form of sermons on the web at <www.thegospelaccordingtolarry.com>, not revealing his true identity. When "Larry" becomes a national phenomenon, Josh's troubles begin.

  • Inside Scoop: "This book has several 'LOL' moments as Josh deals with the ordinary problems of a high school student and the burdens of becoming a nationwide hit." - Mrs. Napier
