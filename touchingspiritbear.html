Touching Spirit Bear

Author: Ben Mikaelsen

Published: 2001

Genre: Adventure, Fantasy/Sci-Fi

AR Points: 9

Description: Cole Matthews has been fighting and stealing for years. But this time he smashed Peter Driscal's head into the sidewalk and is in for a year of exile on an Alaskan Island. Whilst there he is mauled by a mysterious white bear of Native American legend and then has time to think over his life while waiting helplessly alone.

  • Inside Scoop: "A very great book, I wasn't able to put it down. Some of the stuff in it seems like it would relate to a lot of teens today." - Greg Garner
