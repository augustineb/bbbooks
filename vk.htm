Vampire Knight

Author: Matsuri Hino

Published: 2006

Genre: Comic/Manga, Fantasy/Sci-Fi, Drama, Romance

AR Points: None

Author's Website: Matsuri Hino

Description: Cross Academy is a prestigious boarding school home to two separate classes: the Day Class, and the Night Class. The former is full of normal people, but the latter... is composed entirely of vampires. It's up to the chairman's adopted daughter Yuki and her partner Zero to keep this fact a secret, but Zero's got a few dark secrets of his own...

  • The Inside Scoop: "Very intense and engaging! The characters and story are wonderfully composed, and the artwork is gorgeous." - Alyssa Johnson
