Wicked Lovely

Author: Melissa Marr

Published: 2007

Genre: Romance, Fantasy/Sci-Fi, Drama

AR Points: 11

Author's Website: Wicked Lovely

Description: Aislinn has always seen the faeries that roam the streets around her home. They haven’t known that she can see them. The things Ash sees the faeries do to each other are horrible. Her grandmother has always taught her to not acknowledge them. When one of the faeries starts following her around she doesn’t know why. She turns to her best friend, Seth, to help her figure it out.

  • The Inside Scoop: "Wicked Lovely is a book with a little of everything in it. It has a little action, a little romance, a little fantasy, and a mystery. Wicked Lovely is a book that would probably be higher on the list for girls." - Allison Aubert
